About RESS-Resume bank

The RESS resume bank is a student initiative that aims gap the bridge between industry and our student body. By creating an account and uploading your resume our numerous sponsors and industry partners will have a chance to review and contact you if a match is found.

Your Privacy is very important for us, that is why the only people who would have access to your information would be you and potential employers.

If you have any further questions or concerns please contact VpCorps@ress.ca
